=== Operator ===

The Omnia operator is running and watching for:
  - AgentRuntimes
  - PromptPacks
  - ToolRegistries
  - Providers

To verify the operator is running:

  kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "omnia.name" . }}

To deploy your first agent, create an AgentRuntime resource:

  kubectl apply -f - <<EOF
  apiVersion: omnia.altairalabs.ai/v1alpha1
  kind: AgentRuntime
  metadata:
    name: my-agent
  spec:
    promptPackRef:
      name: my-promptpack
    facade:
      type: websocket
      port: 8080
  EOF
